## Lumos

> _Lumos_ — the charm that makes light. Here, sunlight becomes charge, and charge becomes
> something you can see and control.

Solar-Connect is the software half of a solar-powered charging-station thesis: a full-stack
web app wired into the embedded firmware running on the hardware.

## Problem

The hardware works — but a charging station is only useful if people can see status, manage
sessions, and trust the data. The thesis needed a software layer over the firmware.

## My role

Full-stack web development and the **web ↔ firmware integration**: turning device telemetry
into a real-time, human-readable interface.

## Stack & integration

- Full-stack web application (frontend + backend).
- Integration with the **embedded firmware** on the charging hardware.
- Real-time device data surfaced to the UI.

## Outcome

A functioning web layer over the solar charging station, bridging firmware and people.
