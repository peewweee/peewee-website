## The crystal ball

> _Divination_ — gazing into raw numbers to reveal what they mean.

Aura reads the unstructured way people actually record money — quick notes, pasted
receipts, half-sentences — and turns it into structured, queryable financial data with
personalized insight on top.

## Problem

People capture spending as unstructured text and never convert it into something they can
act on. The hard part is the bridge from **raw input → structured data → decision**.

## My role

Full-stack and AI engineering: the ingestion pipeline, the Gemini-backed structuring layer
(unstructured → typed records), caching with Redis, and the insights API.

## How it works

- **Spring Boot** service layer with **PostgreSQL** for durable structured storage.
- **Gemini API** parses unstructured input into typed transactions and generates insights.
- **Redis** caches model responses and hot reads to keep latency and cost down.

## Outcome

Messy input becomes structured records and personalized insights, available on demand.
