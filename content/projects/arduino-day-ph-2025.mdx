## The Room of Requirement

> _The Room of Requirement_ appears as exactly what you need. This site had to become
> exactly what the event needed — clear, fast, and on time.

The official website for **Arduino Day PH 2025**, designed by a volunteer team I led.

## Problem

A national community event needs a site that makes the essentials obvious — schedule,
speakers, registration — and it has to ship on a volunteer timeline.

## My role

**UI/UX Lead.** I led the design volunteers and worked directly with the web development
team to take the site from concept to a shipped product.

## What I did

- Directed the visual design and information architecture of the event site.
- Coordinated a team of design volunteers (handoff, consistency, review).
- Collaborated with web developers on implementation and design QA.

## Outcome

An official, shipped event website delivered on deadline by a volunteer team.
